Career Aspirations

This indicator describes the degree to which a youth has ideas about what type of job they would want or a clear idea of a career direction.

Questions to Consider

  • Does the youth have goals for their job or career development?
  • Is the youth able to identify a job or career path, and do they have resources needed to get there?

Ratings and Descriptions

  • 0 – Clear and feasible career plans.
  • 1 – Career plans exist, but significant barriers may exist to achieving them.
  • 2 – Wants to work but lacks a clear idea of jobs or careers.
  • 3 – No career plans or aspirations.

Job Attendance

This indicator describes the youth’s ability to consistently make it to work based on their job history.

Questions to Consider

  • Has the youth experienced communication or disciplinary action for work attendance issues?
  • Is the youth meeting expectations for attendance?

Ratings and Descriptions

  • 0 – Goes to work consistently as scheduled.
  • 1 – Occasional problems with attendance (e.g., calling in sick when not ill).
  • 2 – Difficulty consistently going to work.
  • 3 – Severe attendance problems that threaten termination or have resulted in recent firing.

Job Performance

This indicator describes the youth’s prior performance based on their job history.

Question to Consider

  • What feedback has the youth received regarding their job performance?

Ratings and Descriptions

  • 0 – Productive employee.
  • 1 – Generally productive, but some performance issues exist.
  • 2 – Problems performing adequately on the job.
  • 3 – Severe performance problems that threaten termination or have resulted in recent firing.

Job Relations

This indicator describes the youth’s history of relationships in work environments.

Question to Consider

  • Are the youth’s relationships at the job setting a source of distress or strength?

Ratings and Descriptions

  • 0 – Gets along well with superiors and co-workers.
  • 1 – Some problems with relationships at work.
  • 2 – Relationship difficulties causing functioning problems at work.
  • 3 – Severe relationship problems that threaten employment or have resulted in recent firing.
